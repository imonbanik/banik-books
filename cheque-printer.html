Cheque Printer

Enter the cheque details on the left. The white cheque canvas on the right updates instantly and prints at the exact cheque size you gave: 7.6 inches by 3.5 inches.

The preview prints the date as DD MM YYYY.
For cash cheques, leave this blank and the preview will show `== CASH ==`.
The printed format follows the style `= 50,000.00`.
`A/C Payee` shows the upper-left stamp. `Cash` leaves that corner blank.
Choose the same direction you place the cheque leaf in the printer. Print preview will open in that orientation.
